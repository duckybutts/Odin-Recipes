Broke Chicken

Recipe:

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Any Great Value Seasoning
  • A bell pepper

Steps

  1. Pound out/ Season the chicken
  2. Pan sear chicken on low for 5 minutes
  3. Cut chicken in the pan because it's taking too long
  4. Dice the bellpepper
  5. Plate the bellpepper
    6. Put chicken onto of diced bellpepper
  6. Enjoy!
