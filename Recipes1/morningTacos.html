Morning Tacos

Recipe:

Ingredients

  • 4 corn tortillas
  • 1/2 avocado
  • 4oz ribeye steak
  • 1/4 cup cilantro
  • 1/8 cup diced jalapenos

Steps

  1. Grill tortillas till crispy
  2. Cook steak to medium-rare
  3. Mix avocado, cilantro, and jalapaneos into paste
  4. Smear paste on each grilled tortilla
  5. Place 1 oz of thinnly cut steak on each tortilla
  6. Enjoy!
